4 Main Types of Dashboards

Operational Dashboard

Monitor, measure, and manage processes or operations with a shorter or more immediate time scale.
Focus on tracking operational processes.

Strategic Dashboard

Monitoring the long-term company strategy
 with the help of critical success factors
Analyzing and benchmarking a wide range of critical trend-based information, quickly tells a cohesive data story.

Analytical Dashboard

These particular dashboards contain large streams of comprehensive data that allow analysts to drill down and extract insights to help the company to progress at an executive level.

Tactical Dashboard

Information-rich dashboards - best suited to mid-management effectively tracks the performance of a company’s goal.
Analysis and monitor processes conducted by mid-level management, emphasizing the analysis.
