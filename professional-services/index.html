Duration of Service Agreements

A typical service engagement could start with an open-ended retainer agreement of 2 months, this allows us to review current state of business, present findings and make suitable change recommendations. Our suggestions cover wide range of organisation matters including processes, tech adoption, automation tools.

Open-ended Retainer

  • Weekly Meetings sync-ups & consultation

  • On-going strategy discussions

and / or

If your organisation has clear needs, expected deliverables, we could engage via a fixed-scope  contract. In this case it would require detailed discussions with your teams to ensure project feasibility and delivery. The proposal process is expected to take a few weeks.
